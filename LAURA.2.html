About Me

Photo of me:

Hi my name is Laura Arsenault. I am currently a freshman at the University of Maine. I am a marketing major. I have been dancing since I was 2 years old and plan on continuing to dance in college. I started dancing competitively when I was 11 and competed in dance up until my senior year of high school. Along with dance I also love music.
